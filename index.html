Essential Online Safety Tips

01

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for each account. Consider using a password manager to generate and store them securely.

02

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA whenever possible to add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

03

Software Updates

Keep your operating system, browsers, and applications updated to protect against known vulnerabilities.

04

Phishing Awareness

Be cautious of suspicious emails, links, and attachments. Verify the sender before clicking or downloading.

05

Secure Networks

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities. Use a VPN when connecting to public networks.

06

Data Backup

Regularly backup your important data to secure cloud storage or external devices.
